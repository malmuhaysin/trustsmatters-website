02 / LOGO

The logo

The Trusts Matters mark is a padlock formed from circuit traces — the union of security and technology. It always pairs with the wordmark set in Plus Jakarta Sans.

Primary — on light
Reversed — on navy
On neutral surface
Clear space & minimum size
Keep clear space = height of the padlock on all sides. Min digital width: 120px.

✓ Do

  • Use the navy logo on white or light surfaces.
  • Use the white (reversed) logo on navy/dark imagery.
  • Keep generous clear space around the mark.
  • Scale proportionally — never stretch.

✕ Don't

  • Recolor the logo outside brand navy/white.
  • Place the navy logo on busy or dark backgrounds.
  • Add shadows, outlines or effects.
  • Rotate, squash or rearrange the lock and wordmark.

04 / TYPOGRAPHY

Typography

Two typefaces, both free on Google Fonts: Plus Jakarta Sans for headings and Inter for body — a modern, professional, highly legible pairing.

Headings — Plus Jakarta Sans · 700/800
Govern risk. Prove your defenses.
Subheading — Plus Jakarta Sans · 700
An IT partner that builds for the long term
Body — Inter · 400 · 16–17px · line-height 1.6
Trusts Matters helps organizations govern risk, achieve compliance and prove their defenses — with security designed in from the first line of code.
Labels / eyebrows — Plus Jakarta Sans · 700 · uppercase · letter-spacing .14em
What we secure

Type scale

12 · 14 · 16 · 18 · 24 · 32 · 42 px — keep body text at a minimum of 16px.

05 / ICONOGRAPHY & IMAGERY

Icons & imagery

Use a single consistent line-icon set (Lucide / Heroicons) with a 2px stroke. Icons sit in rounded navy or soft-teal tiles. Never use emoji as icons.

Photography & graphics

Favor cool, professional imagery — secure operations, modern architecture, data centers, the Riyadh skyline. Apply a subtle navy overlay for text legibility. Use the faint circuit-grid pattern as a background texture on dark sections only.

06 / VOICE & TONE

Voice & tone

Confident and clear, never alarmist. We speak as a trusted expert who reduces complexity — precise, calm, and outcome-focused.

We are

Clear, authoritative, reassuring, standards-grounded, plain-spoken.

We avoid

Fear-mongering, hype, jargon for its own sake, absolute guarantees.

Say this, not that

✓ "Prove your defenses with testing mapped to real attack paths."
✕ "Hackers are everywhere — you're not safe!"
✓ "Regulator-ready programs mapped to NCA, SAMA and ISO 27001."
✕ "100% unhackable, guaranteed security."
